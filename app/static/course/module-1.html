MODULE 1

First Output — Publish Today

This course is not about theory. It is about output. Momentum beats perfection — every single time.

Your only goal today:
Publish one Short. Even if it feels average. Especially if it feels average.

Step 1 — Pick ONE lane (5 minutes)

  • Choose one niche (motivation, facts, history, AI, finance, etc.)
  • Do NOT switch niches for the next 7 days
  • One lane = faster momentum

Step 2 — Pick ONE format

  • Voice-over clips
  • Caption-only clips
  • Talking head (optional)

You don’t need variety. You need repeatability.

Step 3 — Publish (no editing spiral)

  • Generate clips with MotionForge
  • Pick one that’s “good enough”
  • Post it today — no delay
Rule:
No manual editing unless a clip proves it deserves it.

Non-negotiables

  • 1 upload per day (minimum)
  • No deleting posts
  • No niche hopping

Consistency compounds quietly.
